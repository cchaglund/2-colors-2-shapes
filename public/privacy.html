Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

2 Colors 2 Shapes ("we", "our", or "the app") is a creative daily challenge app. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Email address - Used to identify your account
  • Name and profile picture - Used to display your avatar

We also store:

  • Your chosen nickname - Displayed publicly in the gallery instead of your real name
  • Your creations - The shapes and designs you save

How We Use Your Information

  • To let you save and retrieve your creations
  • To display your nickname (not your email or real name) in the public gallery
  • To enable features like rating other users' creations

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't send marketing emails
  • We don't share your email with third parties
  • We don't track you across other websites

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. Servers are located in the United States.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Sign out at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google Sign-In - For authentication (Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase - For database and authentication infrastructure
  • Netlify - For hosting the website

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
